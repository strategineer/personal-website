---
title: Garlic Pickle Slices
date: 2025-11-30
food/tags:
  - tried-and-true
servings: 12
tools:
  - 1 large jar (1L)
  - 1 medium sauce pan
ingredients:
  - 12-15 small cucumbers
  - 4-5 garlic cloves, peeled and smashed
  - 1 bunch fresh dill sprigs
  - 1 1/2 cups of water
  - 1 1/2 cups of vinegar
  - 2 tbps of kosher salt
  - 1 tbps of sugar
  - 1/2 tsp of red pepper flakes
steps:
  - Wash the cucumbers.
  - Cut the tips of cucumbers and throw them away.
  - Slice the cucumbers into slices length-wise.
  - In a medium sauce pan, mix the white vinegar, water, kosher salt, and sugar.
  - Heat over medium-high until it begins to boil, stirring occasionally until the salt and sugar have completely dissolved.
  - Remove the saucepan from the heat and allow the brine to cool to room temperature.
  - Place the smashed garlic cloves, fresh dill sprigs, and red pepper flakes at the bottom of the jar.
  - Carefully pack the cucumber slices tightly into the jar.
  - Once the brine is cool, pour it into the jar ensuring that the cucumber slices remain in the brine (no floaters!).
  - Seal the jar and let it ferment for 48 hours (burping it daily).
  - Store them in the fridge and enjoy over the course of the next few weeks.
---
