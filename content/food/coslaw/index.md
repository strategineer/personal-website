---
title: "Ideal Coslaw"
date: 2025-10-04
weight: 1
food/tags:
  - tried-and-true
summary: Some slaw.
servings: 12
tools:
  - 3 glass jars
ingredients:
  - 1 medium Taiwanese cabbage, sliced 
  - 3-4 small/medium carrots, sliced
  - 1 cup of mayonaise
  - 1 tbsp of Dijon mustard 
  - 3 tbsp of apple cider vinegar
  - 3 tbsp of maple syrup
  - 1 tsp of garlic powder
  - 1 tsp of onion powder
  - 1/4 tsp of pepper
  - 1/8 tsp of celery seeds 
  - 1/2 tsp of salt (to taste)
steps:
  - Mix sliced cabbage and carrots by hand.
  - Whisk all the other ingredients together.
  - Pour over the cabbage/carrots and mix thoroughly by hand.
  - Enjoy!
  - Leftovers can be stored in airtight jars, refrigerated and eaten over the course of 2-4 weeks.
