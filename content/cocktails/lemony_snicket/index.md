title: "Lemony Snicket"
date: 2023-01-28
cocktails/tags:
  - light
  - gin
weight: 1
blurb:.. 
ingredients:
  - 2 oz gin
  - 1 oz triple sec
  - 1 lemon
  - Club soda
steps:
  - Squeeze the juice from the lemon.
  - Shake and strain all ingredients into an old fashioned glass with some ice cubes.
  - Fill with club soda.
