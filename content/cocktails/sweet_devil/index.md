---
title: "Sweet Devil"
date: 2023-02-06
cocktails/tags:
  - light
  - whiskey
weight: 2
summary: "A fruity sweet and sour drink. A lot of flavors in every sip."
ingredients:
  - 1 oz Jack Daniel's whiskey
  - 1/2 oz Campari 
  - 1 lemon
  - 1/2 oz maple syrup
  - Club soda
steps:
  - Squeeze the juice from the lemon.
  - Shake and strain all ingredients into a highball glass with some ice cubes.
  - Top with club soda.
---
