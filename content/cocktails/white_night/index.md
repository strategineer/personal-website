---
title: "White Night"
date: 2023-02-17
cocktails/tags:
  - light
  - Tequila
weight: 1
summary: Smooth and sweet but not too sweet.
ingredients:
  - 2 oz golden tequila
  - 1 oz orgeat syrup
  - 1 lime
  - 3 drops of Angostura bitters
  - fizzy water
steps:
  - Squeeze the juice from the lime.
  - Shake and strain all ingredients into a big fuck off glass with some ice
    cubes. Top with fizzy water.
---
