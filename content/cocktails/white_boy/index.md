---
title: "White Boy"
date: 2023-01-30
cocktails/tags:
  - light
  - gin
weight: 1
summary: Smooth and sweet but not too sweet.
ingredients:
  - 2 oz Bombay Sapphire gin
  - 1 oz orgeat syrup
  - 1 lime
  - 1/2 oz mint syrup
steps:
  - Squeeze the juice from the lime.
  - Shake and strain all ingredients into an old fashioned glass with some ice
    cubes.
---
